## Market Gap Analysis

The intersection of **"agentic AI engineering" + "cinematic Three.js production" + "vibe coding speed"** is an **empty white space**:

- **Cinematic agencies** (Lusion, Noomo, KOTA) charge $30K-$500K, no AI offering
- **AI builders** (Base44, Lovable, Bolt.new) charge $0-$200/mo, no cinematic quality
- **No mid-tier exists** at $3K-$15K with premium quality + AI-assisted delivery
